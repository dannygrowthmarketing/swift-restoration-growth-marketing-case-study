# 02 · Business Overview

> A trusted, owner-operated emergency-restoration business punching below its digital weight.

## Executive Summary

Swift Restoration LLC is a Boone, Iowa restoration company (founded 2018, owner Austin Moorman) serving residential and commercial clients across eight central-Iowa counties with 24/7 emergency response. The business has strong fundamentals — reputation, responsiveness, insurance fluency — and an underdeveloped digital presence.

## Business Problem

Emergency restoration is a **moment-of-crisis, high-intent purchase**. Customers search at the moment of damage and call the first credible provider that appears. Swift's offline strengths don't surface in those moments because the digital assets that win them (local pages, profile, reviews, paid presence) are missing or generic.

## Research Findings

- **Services (current + planned):** water damage (flood, sewer, mold), fire/smoke/odor, carpet/upholstery, HVAC cleaning, junk/demolition, interior refinishing — plus to-be-built standalone **mold remediation, storm damage, sewage/biohazard, commercial, and emergency** lines.
- **Service area (8 counties):** Boone, Story, Polk, Dallas, Webster, Hamilton, Greene, Guthrie — a rural home base (Boone) bleeding into the Des Moines metro.
- **Model:** emergency lead generation; insurance-billed jobs; recurring B2B potential (property managers).
- **Reputation:** 5.0★ across 15 Google reviews; praised for response speed, professionalism, and insurance handling.

## Strategic Recommendation

Position Swift explicitly as the **fast, local, owner-operated alternative to franchises** — a message that resonates in Boone/Ames and differentiates from generic national branding. Anchor everything in the 24/7 response promise and the insurance-handling competency customers already praise.

## Implementation Plan

Codify the service taxonomy and service-area list as the backbone of the new site architecture (Sections 08–09). Capture the brand promise as repeatable on-page and ad messaging (Sections 11, 14, 15).

## Expected Business Impact

A clear, differentiated market position that converts local trust into search and map visibility, and gives every page and ad a consistent, conversion-oriented message.

## KPIs

Branded vs. non-branded search visibility, share of local-pack presence in home-turf towns, lead volume by service line and county.

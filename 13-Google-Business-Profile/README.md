# 13 · Google Business Profile

> The single highest-leverage lever for a local emergency business.

## Executive Summary

Swift's GBP is its strongest latent asset (5.0★/15 reviews) and most under-optimized channel. A structured optimization + review engine compounds both local rankings and conversion — and unlocks Local Services Ads.

## Business Problem

For emergency restoration, GBP review volume/velocity and profile completeness often outweigh on-site SEO for local-pack ranking and for the click-to-call decision. Swift's profile is good but passive.

## Research Findings

5.0★ on 15 reviews; categories centered on Water Damage Restoration / Carpet / HVAC; reviews specifically praise speed and insurance handling — strong conversion proof currently under-displayed on-site and under-grown.

## Strategic Recommendation

- **Optimize:** primary + secondary categories (water/fire/mold/storm), full services list, service-area, hours, attributes, weekly photos, and GBP Posts.
- **Review engine:** systematic post-job review requests (SMS/email) to lift count and **velocity**; respond to every review.
- **Q&A:** seed and answer common emergency questions.
- **Verification:** ensure GBP verification (the blue Verified badge) — a **prerequisite for LSA** (Section 14).
- **Surface proof on-site:** embed reviews on service/location pages.

## Implementation Plan

Stand up the review workflow in Month 1; monthly posting/photo cadence; verification confirmed before LSA launch.

## Expected Business Impact

Higher local-pack rankings, higher click-to-call conversion from social proof, and an unlocked LSA channel — the three compounding the same reputation asset.

## KPIs

Review count + velocity, average rating (hold ≥4.9), GBP views/calls/direction requests, verification status, % of service/location pages displaying reviews.

# 18 · Month-1 Roadmap

> Thirty days: foundation, profile, first money pages, paid fixes, tracking.

## Executive Summary

Month 1 establishes the technical and measurement foundation, activates the highest-leverage local lever (GBP/reviews), ships the first conversion pages (Boone + Ames water/emergency), and fixes paid media — sequenced by dependency.

## Business Problem

With limited resources, sequencing matters: do the unblocking work first (migration foundation, tracking, GBP verification) so later work compounds instead of stalling.

## Research Findings

Dependencies: migration foundation unblocks clean pages; GBP verification unblocks LSA; tracking unblocks ROI proof; Boone/Ames first per competitive analysis.

## Strategic Recommendation

**Week 1 — Foundation & quick wins**
- Lock migration SEO spec + 301 map (Section 07); rewrite homepage title/meta/H1.
- Fix the Google Ads `&amp;` bug; consolidate to 2–3 campaigns.
- Stand up GA4 + call tracking + GSC.

**Week 2 — Local lever**
- GBP optimization + verification; launch the review-request engine.
- Begin citation/NAP cleanup; resolve the duplicate-domain ownership question.

**Week 3 — First money pages**
- Ship Water Damage + Emergency service pillars and **Boone + Ames** location pages (templated, schema, click-to-call).

**Week 4 — Paid expansion & proof**
- Launch **LSA** in Boone/Ames (post-verification); matched landing pages live; capture baseline KPIs.

## Implementation Plan

Run as a dependency-ordered sprint; each item has an owner and an acceptance check (e.g., staging crawl, verification confirmed, tracking firing).

## Expected Business Impact

By day 30: clean foundation, verified + optimized profile with rising reviews, the first high-conversion pages live, paid spend efficient, and a real measurement baseline.

## KPIs

Items shipped vs. plan, GBP verification + review velocity, pages live + indexed, LSA live, baseline KPIs captured.

Targets to measure against in [19](../19-Results-and-KPIs/README.md); beyond 30 days in [20](../20-Future-Roadmap/README.md).

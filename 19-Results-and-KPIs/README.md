# 19 · Results & KPIs

> Measured by business outcomes — calls and leads — not rankings alone.

## Executive Summary

This engagement defines success as qualified lead growth, with a baseline-and-target dashboard. Several baselines are being established as the analytics stack goes live; the metrics already measured are shown as real.

## Business Problem

Local SEO is often reported in vanity rankings. Swift needs ROI evidence: more qualified calls at an efficient cost.

## Research Findings

Measured today: Domain Rating 3.2; GBP 5.0★/15 reviews; ~7 real indexed pages. To be baselined via Section 17: organic sessions, leads, calls, GBP views/calls, local rankings, referring domains.

## Strategic Recommendation

**Baseline → 30-day → 90-day (illustrative targets; bands until live data):**

| KPI | Baseline | 30-day | 90-day |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tracked phone calls | not yet tracked | tracking live + baseline | upward trend |
| Organic + LSA leads | not yet tracked | LSA live + baseline | upward trend |
| GBP views / calls | to capture | baseline captured | growth |
| Reviews (count / rating) | 15 / 5.0★ | 25 / ≥4.9★ | 45+ / ≥4.9★ |
| Local-pack rank (Boone/Ames core) | ~none | entering | top-3 (Boone core) |
| Indexed real pages | ~7 | ~10 | ~17+ |
| Domain Rating | 3.2 | maintain | slight ↑ |

Targets are deliberately framed as outcomes and refined once GA4/call-tracking data lands.

## Implementation Plan

Populate the live KPI dashboard (companion workbook tab) monthly; report cost-per-qualified-lead by channel.

## Expected Business Impact

A defensible, ROI-oriented results narrative suitable for the client and for this portfolio — grounded in measured data, honest about what is still being baselined.

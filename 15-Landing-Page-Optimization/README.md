# 15 · Landing Page Optimization

> Emergency-intent pages engineered for one action: call now.

## Executive Summary

Paid and organic emergency traffic needs purpose-built landing pages that match the query, prove trust instantly, and make calling effortless — not a generic homepage.

## Business Problem

Sending high-intent emergency clicks to a placeholder homepage depresses conversion and wastes paid spend; message mismatch between ad and page erodes Quality Score and trust.

## Research Findings

Today there are no dedicated service/location landing pages; the homepage leads with emotion, not action, and lacks above-fold click-to-call, town relevance, and trust signals.

## Strategic Recommendation

Per service × town landing page:
- **Above-fold:** service + town in H1, click-to-call button, 24/7 + response-time promise.
- **Trust:** 5.0★ rating, review count, IICRC/insurance signals, real local photos.
- **Message-match** to the ad/keyword that drove the click.
- **Short form** (name, phone, issue) as call alternative; sticky mobile call bar.
- **Speed + mobile-first** (Core Web Vitals budget).

## Implementation Plan

Build templated landing pages on .NET tied to the location/service taxonomy; map each ad group/LSA category to its matched page.

## Expected Business Impact

Higher conversion rate and Quality Score, lower cost-per-lead, and more calls from the same traffic.

## KPIs

Landing-page conversion rate, click-to-call rate, form completion, ad Quality Score, cost per lead by page.

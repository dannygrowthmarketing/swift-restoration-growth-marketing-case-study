# 16 · Conversion Rate Optimization

> Convert Swift's offline trust into phone calls.

## Executive Summary

CRO here is about removing friction from a single decision made under stress: call this company now. The levers are trust, speed-of-contact, and reassurance.

## Business Problem

Even with traffic, weak calls-to-action, buried phone numbers, and missing trust signals leak hard-won emergency demand.

## Research Findings

The current site under-uses its strongest conversion assets (5.0★ reputation, 24/7 response, insurance handling) and lacks prominent, persistent calling mechanisms.

## Strategic Recommendation

- **Click-to-call everywhere** (sticky header + mobile bar).
- Lead with the **24/7 + ~60-minute response** promise and **5.0★** proof.
- **Insurance reassurance** ("we document everything and work with your adjuster").
- **Social proof placement** near every CTA; located case studies as evidence.
- **Reduce form fields**; offer call as primary, form as fallback.
- **A/B test** hero messaging, CTA copy, and proof placement once traffic supports it.

## Implementation Plan

Bake these into the .NET templates so every page inherits them; instrument call/form events (Section 17) to measure lift.

## Expected Business Impact

More leads per visit across organic, paid, and LSA — improving ROI on every other channel simultaneously.

## KPIs

Overall lead conversion rate, click-to-call rate, form completion, call quality, cost per lead.
